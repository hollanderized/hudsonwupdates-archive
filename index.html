following the recent deactivations of hudson williams updates (@hudsonwupdates on twitter) —twice in less than a month— and out of concern that the early months of hudson williams' career and fandom history could eventually be lost permanently, this archive was created in order to preserve those first months of activity.

this project exists purely for archival purposes. the original account has done an incredible job documenting hudson's early career, and this archive would not exist without that work. i am deeply grateful for the time, care, and dedication that went into preserving and sharing these moments as they happened. however, the repeated deactivations have made me, and many fans, worried about the long-term preservation of this content, and i did not want to risk losing such an important record of these early stages of hudson's career and public history. this archive was created to ensure that these early moments remain accessible and are not lost over time.

this archive only contains publicly accessible content originally shared online through public social media posts. all media featured in this archive belongs to its original photographers, creators, and posters. this project exists solely for archival and preservation purposes and does not claim ownership over any content included here.

the archive currently includes posts that were publicly available as of may 8th, 2026. while every effort was made to preserve as much content as possible, some posts or media may still be missing. i do not currently plan to continue updating it over time, as other update accounts have since begun documenting hudson's career and preserving these moments as well.

the archive is organized chronologically by month, with posts further grouped by week and day in order to make navigation easier and preserve the sense of timeline and progression surrounding hudson's early career and fandom history. this structure was chosen both for accessibility and to better reflect the way these moments were originally experienced in real time.

finally, i'm not a web developer, so apologies for how simple this archive is! if you have suggestions, corrections, or ideas that could improve the project, feel free to contact me on twitter @hollanderized.
